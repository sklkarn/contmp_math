Theorem 4.5.9.

If \(n\) and \(r\) are positive integers such that \(1\leq r\leq n,\) then we have
  1. \begin{equation*} \frac{^nC_{r}}{^nC_{(r-1)}} = \frac{n-r+1}{r} \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} \frac{^nC_{r}}{^{(n-1)}C_{(r-1)}} = \frac{n}{r} \end{equation*}
  3. \begin{equation*} \frac{^nC_{r}}{^nC_{(r+1)}} = \frac{r+1}{n-r}. \end{equation*}
